About Us

As filmmakers, we know what it takes to make a film. With SquareEyes we aim to give a platform and voice to independent filmmakers who are overlooked by a vast majority of distributors.

We know there's an audience out there for every film, and instead of waiting for the traditional Hollywood system to include us, we've created our own ecosystem.
